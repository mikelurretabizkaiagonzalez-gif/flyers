// Our Mission

AVIATION
FOR ALL

Today there is a growing distrust towards aviation. People talk about more accidents, dangerous turbulences — and that creates fear. But the reality is very different from what is perceived.

Our mission is to introduce society to the world of aviation, share basic knowledge, eliminate that insecurity and generate a real positive impact.

"Gizarteari mundu honetan sartaraztea irtenbidea da — abiazioak ikuspegi oso interesgarri bat du."

// Why it matters

POSITIVE
IMPACT

Flyers is more than an app. It's a project designed to make the world a little better — eliminating fear and opening doors to a fascinating sector that moves millions of people every single day.

  • ✓Reduce irrational fear of flying with truthful, accessible information
  • ✓Bring the aviation world closer to young people and adults without technical background
  • ✓Offer a premium service with social impact as the primary objective
  • ✓Build a community of people passionate about aviation
  • ✓Make flying more affordable through exclusive flight discounts
